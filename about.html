My Story

My name is David, I'm from the hills of the Appalachian Mountains. Born and raised in the Eastern Ky region.


My story starts out from skimming on my scanner, looking for new freq to add and listen to. As I was scanning I came across a local (Friendship Net) Confused to what it was I set and monitored it. Looked up the freq and it came to a local repeater system I didn't know we had.

I looked online to what a friendship net was, came out to be a place where people would check it around a set time and had conversation on the repeater. Curious I dug deeper into the world of (ham radio) Studied, looked at other hams on this very website. Seen shared stories of contacts from all over the world.

Purchased my first radio (Baofeng UV-5R) and learned how to program the local repeater, The more I monitored it the more I wanted to join.

From that moment on I studied and studied, Still learning more as we speak. took my test and passed both my tecnichian and general on the same day. I am currently saving to buy an HF rig before I get my Extra class.

That is my story of how I became apart of this family. May god bless you all and give me a new passion.

73 de KN4KAE
